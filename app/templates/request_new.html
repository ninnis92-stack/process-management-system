Writing a great request

Follow these simple steps to make sure your request moves smoothly:

  1. Start with context. Provide a concise title and a detailed description of the goal, background, and any blockers.
  2. Part information first. Enter donor and target part numbers so the system routes and verifies correctly; you can update later if unsure.
  3. Pick a realistic due date. Give enough lead time for reviews or collaboration with other departments.
  4. Use helper hints. Look at the cards at the top while filling out the form – they contain the most common tips.
  5. Attach files or photos. When dynamic fields are configured, you can upload images or documents directly; OCR will attempt to extract text automatically.
